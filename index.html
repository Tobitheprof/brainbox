    • Helping members grow and thrive in the space by encouraging the constant individual research / intel hunting
    • Providing a supportive and engaging community where each members can have high quality conversation and build up solid relationships
    • To be well versed in the space thereby giving a chance for both old and newbies and also providing them with tools, resources and scopes to help scale up their game in the space
